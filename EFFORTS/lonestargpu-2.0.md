- **lonestar-bfs-atomic**, **lonestar-bfs-wlw**, **lonestar-bfs-wla**, <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">**lonestar-bfs-wlc**</mark> (variants of BFS)

- **lonestar-dmr** (diffusion), **lonestar-mst** (minimum spanning tree)
